Welcome to Phishing Awareness

This website is dedicated to spreading awareness about phishing and educating users about the dangers of phishing attacks.

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details, by disguising as a trustworthy entity.

My goal is to provide information, tips, and resources to help you recognize phishing attempts, protect your personal information, and stay safe online.

Explore the content and learn how to defend yourself against phishing scams. Remember, knowledge is your best defense!
