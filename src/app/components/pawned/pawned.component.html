Dangers of Phishing

Phishing is a Serious Threat!

Phishing is a malicious attempt to deceive individuals into revealing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data. It's important to understand the dangers associated with phishing:

  • Your personal and financial information can be stolen.
  • Your accounts can be compromised.
  • Your identity can be used for fraudulent activities.
  • Malware can be installed on your device.
  • Your reputation can be damaged.

Protect Yourself:

  • Always verify the sender's email address.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Keep your software and security tools up to date.
  • Be cautious and skeptical of unsolicited requests for personal information.
